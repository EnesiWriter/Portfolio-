01 Origin

I started
with words.

Before analytics dashboards and content calendars, there was just me and a blank page. Growing up, I found that stories were the most honest way to make sense of the world — and the most powerful way to move people within it.

I studied Agriculture at the University of Ilorin — graduating with a 4.24 GPA — but my real education was happening in the margins: writing short fiction at night, submitting to literary journals, slowly learning what it meant to hold a reader's attention.

Then something clicked. I realised that the same craft that makes a reader turn a page — tension, voice, emotional truth, the right detail at the right moment — is exactly what makes someone stop scrolling.

That realisation changed everything.

01

Hydrogen Africa

Social Media Manager · Lagos, Nigeria
2023 — Present

When I joined Hydrogen Payment Services, the challenge was sharp: build a social presence for a fintech brand in a crowded market, from scratch, with zero budget. Most people would have called that an impossible brief.

I called it a story problem.

The insight was simple: businesses in Nigeria aren't just looking for a payment gateway — they're looking for a partner they can trust. So instead of leading with features, I led with empathy. Polls that asked real questions about their pain points. Quote cards that spoke to their daily realities. Product posts that felt like reassurance, not advertising.

Within 30 days, engagement was up 40%. Within months, brand visibility had risen 70% and lead generation by 45%.

In Q1, social audience grew by 27%. The newsletter reached and sustained 10,000+ active subscribers. The content felt like the writing. It had a voice.
